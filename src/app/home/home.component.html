About Us

North Ammunition Industries is one of Lebanon’s leading manufacturers of well built, reliable cartridges for the hunting society.
NAI was established in 2010 in north Lebanon, Besebhel.

We've been serving the highest cartridge quality all over Lebanon. Due to your trust and encouragement , our team in North Ammunition Industries decided to extend the company by providing a bigger location with a greater productivity .

Our products are designed to help progress and develop the shooters performance to achieve far reaching results. All of our shots are made to rigorous quality control standards to ensure the highest quality at all time .
